Admission

I welcome motivated students to carry out PhD research under my supervision in the area of network science, swarming phenomena, dynamical systems, or a closely related field. Interested students are encouraged to first contact me with your CV that includes GPA, and then apply to the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology. I may offer you a Research Assistantship, which is enough to cover your tuition, depending on my funding.
