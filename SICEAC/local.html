Conference venue - Nara Kasugano International Forum

Nara is an ancient capital city in Nara Prefecture, Japan. Its 1,300 years of cultural history as Japan's first permanent capital attracts more than a million visitors every year. Besides being home to many important scenic and historical sites, Nara is located less than one hour from Kyoto and Osaka. https://www.visitnara.jp/

The SICE Annual Conference 2018 (SICE 2018) will be held at Nara Kasugano International Forum. The conference site is near several historic treasures, including Todai-ji temple and Kasuga-taisha shrine.

Getting Nara is easy with frequent and convenient shuttle service from the following two major airports in Kansai region; Kansai International Airport (KIX) and Osaka International Airport (ITM). To know how to get to Nara from other places, visit https://www.visitnara.jp/travel-to-nara/

Directions

The closest and most convenient station to the conference venue is the Kintetsu Nara Station. From the station, you can either walk to or take a bus to the conference venue.
