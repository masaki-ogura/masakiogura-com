Masaki Ogura is a Postdoctoral Researcher in Electrical and Systems Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania. His primary research interest lies in the analysis of dynamical systems over complex and dynamical networks. He received his Ph.D. degree in Mathematics from Texas Tech University.
